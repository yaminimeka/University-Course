This Course Registeration System will allow the students to register their classes for the semester. In this system student will login with his username and Password which is provided to him by the university.
                The student can check for tha available CRN's and can select his classes,professors and timings of the classes.
Firstly the student will be directed to a login page where in he can enter his login details and will be redirected to the welcome page where in he has a tab for checking CRN's .After selecting the respective classes and professors he will be enrolled in the classes.
